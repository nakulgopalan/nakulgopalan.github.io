Nakul Gopalan

I am a graduate student in Stefanie Tellex's H2R lab at Brown. I am interested in the problem of grounding language to tasks specifications. I want to develop robots that can be trained by people with a few demonstrations using natural langauge. This would improve the usability of robots within homes and offices. My other interests are in hierarchical reinforcement learning and planning.
