Nakul Gopalan

I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Computing and Artificial Intelligence (SCAI) within Arizona State University. My research interests lie at the intersection of language grounding and robot learning. I lead the Logos Robotics Lab at ASU. I am interested in algorithms and methods that allow robots to be trained by leveraging demonstrations and natural language descriptions. Such learning would improve the usability of robots within homes and offices. My other research interests are in hierarchical reinforcement learning and planning.
